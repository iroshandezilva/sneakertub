Our Story

One of the greatest joys that a sneakerhead feels is when a package arrives in the mail. Whether you know what is inside the package or not, the joy comes from opening up that package, and then opening up the shoebox to see the treasure that lies within. If it happens to be a package where you don’t know the contents inside, it brings elements of surprise and excitement that adds to the experience. SNEAKERTUB IS LIKE CHRISTMAS... But it's every month so let's call it KICKS-MAS.

SNEAKERTUB is the world's first sneaker subscription service which delivers curated mystery sneaker packages to our subscribers. We work with the best brands in the world to deliver a unique sneaker experience to our members.
